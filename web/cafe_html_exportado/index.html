THE FINEST COFFEE & TEA

 

We serve only premium roast coffees from around the world and offer fresh coffee for each customer. We also offer a selection of loose leaf teas, everything from exotic flavors to the classics.

DELICIOUS HOME BAKING

 

Based on Katie’s original award-winning recipes, we serve a full menu of baked goods. These items are made fresh each morning to ensure you are getting the best quality possible.

FRESH & HEALTHY MEALS

 

With an extensive breakfast, lunch, and dinner menu, we do more than your typical café. All of our meals are made to order and contain only the freshest ingredients.
